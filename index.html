/about

I'm a web-developer living in Gurgaon, India. Much of my work has been across a modern-day Javascript stack, that usually comprises:

  • MongoDB for storage and Mongoose for the wiring
  • Node.js and Express on the server-side
  • Angular.js on the client-side
  • ZURB's Foundation 5 for the front-end

Off-work, I like experimenting with canvas graphics using algorithms that simulate life and organic matter, like Lindenmayer trees, Metaballs and Boids -- the interactive creatures that you see swimming in the background on this website.
